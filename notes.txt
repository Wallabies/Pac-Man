Notes for your convenience!

Data Types:
	0  - Empty, completely blank, not important, just a place holder
	1  - Small coin
	2  - Large coin (energizer)
	3  - Normal wall
	4  - Border wall
	5  - Cell wall
	6  - Cell door
	7  - Empty, but a solid block
	8  - Tunnel, renders as empty
	82 - 'R' character
	69 - 'E' character
	65 - 'A' character
	68 - 'D' character
	89 - 'Y' character
	33 - '!' character
